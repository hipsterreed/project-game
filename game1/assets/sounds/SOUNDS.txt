PLACEHOLDER SOUND MANIFEST
==========================

The game runs entirely on procedurally synthesized audio out of the box —
nothing in this folder is required. Drop in real recordings with the exact
filenames below and the audio engine will use them instead. Anything missing
silently falls back to the procedural version.

Recommended format: .ogg (loops cleanest in browsers). .mp3 also works.
For looping channels (wind/leaves/music), make sure the file loops seamlessly.

CHANNELS
--------

ambient_wind.ogg
    Long looping wind layer. Mid/low band, gusty.
    Length: 30–90 seconds.
    What it does in-engine: base ambience, gets louder as you walk faster.

ambient_leaves.ogg
    Higher, brighter rustle / leaves layer. Loops with wind.
    Length: 20–60 seconds.

music_drone.ogg
    The foundation tone. Always playing once started. Should be a low,
    sustained pad / drone in A (suggested A2 / 110 Hz). No melody.
    Length: 60+ seconds, seamless loop.

music_bed_low.ogg
    Mid-range pad layer. Fades in as the player walks deeper into the world
    (drives the "progress" channel). Suggest a fifth (E) above the drone.
    Length: 60+ seconds, seamless loop.

music_bed_mid.ogg
    Movement layer — fades in when the player is moving. Should feel like
    energy/motion (warmer pad, soft strings). A major triad works.
    Length: 60+ seconds, seamless loop.

music_bed_high.ogg
    Shimmer layer that only enters when the player is BOTH moving and
    deep into the journey. Bright, airy (high pad, bells, choir). The
    "emotional payoff" timbre.
    Length: 60+ seconds, seamless loop.

music_swell.ogg
    One-shot. Plays exactly once when the player arrives at the lone tree.
    Should feel like resolution / homecoming. ~10 seconds with a long tail.

footstep_grass.ogg
    A single short footstep on grass. Engine pitch-shifts and re-triggers
    automatically; provide one clean sample.
    Length: 0.2–0.4 seconds.

bird_chirp.ogg
    A single bird call. Engine triggers occasionally with random pitch.
    Length: 0.3–1.5 seconds.


HOW THE ADAPTIVE MIX WORKS
--------------------------

  speed   ─►  wind volume, bedMid volume, bedHigh volume, melody trigger rate
  progress ─► bedLow volume, bedHigh volume, fewer melody notes (more focused)
  arrival ─► music_swell fires once, particle burst from the tree

That means: standing still = quiet drone + wind. Walking = full bed builds.
Approaching the tree = high shimmer joins. Arriving = swell + payoff.

To swap in real audio: just save the .ogg files into this folder.
